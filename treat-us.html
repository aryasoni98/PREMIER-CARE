Orthopedic Physical Therapy


At Liberty Physical Therapy we understand how fascinating and powerful the human body is. It has a strong potential to heal but needs the appropriate intervention to manifest this healing energy. With a personalized treatment plan, we help channel this energy to restore your health in its highest form.

Based on our careful evaluation of your symptom, we custom tailor a treatment plan for you including both, hands-on approach and exercises
We provide ongoing evaluation and feedback to adjust your treatment as you progress and share these changes with you and your physician
We also provide you with a regimen that you can use at home for long-term recovery and pain-free living
With every step we move closer to our ultimate goal of your complete recovery and restoration to every day activities

Experience Un-Matched Expertise: Board-Certified Clinical Specialist Orthopedic Physical Therapist at Your Service
