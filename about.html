About PREMIER CARE

Conveniently located on Oak Tree Road in Edison, Premier Care Physical Therapy & Rehabilitation is operated by Paramjit (PJ) a Board-Certified Clinical Specialist in Orthopedic Physical Therapy, a prestigious certification earned only by 5% of Physical Therapist in USA. Our clinic strive on providing exceptional one-one-one personalized care to each one of our patient which you may not find easily in other physical therapy clinics. Our highly skilled & experienced therapist focus on accurate diagnosis of the problem which prevent wasting precious time on the wrong course of treatment and thus ensure speedy recovery of all our patients. At Premier Care Physical Therapy & Rehabilitation, our ultimate goal is to optimize physical function, promote well-being, and help individuals regain their highest level of independence and quality of life through evidence-based interventions and compassionate care.

Our Philosophy

Our philosophy behind the personalized care that we strive for grew from knowing that every person is an individual and needs customized & tailored treatment plan for their specific case, we believe this is the best way to ensure our patients receive the long-term relief they deserve. Our focus is on finding results that last so that you can get back to life. And this starts by identifying what is the best course forward for you, specifically. Anyone who has been struggling with chronic pain for years knows finding long-term relief can truly be an uphill battle, often they don’t find a long term solution without risk of invasive surgery or reliance on prescription pain medications. If this sounds like you, then it’s time to rethink your options and your approach.

Patient Education

The other area we really focus on is empowering our patients to become more aware of their condition or illness by Patient Education. Examples of Patient education include home exercise programs, ergonomics instruction, body mechanics training and injury prevention. Patient Education also enables patients to make right choices and help them take charge of their own health.
